Tutorial for TZ Liker

Step 1: Set up your Facebook account

Step 1.1: Unlock your profile

You can skip this step if you have already unlocked your facebook profile. First of all, open any browser and go to facebook.com. Then log into your facebook account if you haven't already.


Then you will see 3 bar menu icon, click on it. Scroll down and click on Setiings,


Then scroll down a little bit and click on Profile Locking.


Now click on Unlock and a new page will open up. Click on Unlock your profile on that page.



You have successfully unlocked your facebook profile. Now follow the next steps

Step 1.2: Make your Profile Public

Go to facebook.com, click on the 3 bar menu and go to Settings. 


Now, under Audience & Visibility click on Followers and Public Content.


Now click on Public under Who Can Follow Me.


Then click on Public under Public Post Comments.


And the last one, click on Public under Public Profile Info.


Now you have successfully set upped your facebook account for auto liker. Follow the next steps.

TIP: Refresh the page and to make sure the changes submitted to the server.

Step 2: Register on TZ Liker

You can skip this step if you have already registered on TZ Liker. Follow the next step to Connect your facebook account with TZ Liker.

At first, go to register page and fill up the form. Then click on register.


After registering, you will be redirected to login page. There, fill up the form and click on Login.


If your information was correct you will be logged in and redirected to Dashboard. Now here click on Auto Like or Auto Follow. You will be redirected and forced to set your facebook cookie to connect your account with this site.


Follow the next step to get your facebook cookie and set it up.

Step 3: Connect your Facebook Account

 To connect your facebook account, you need to set your facebook cookie.

Let's see how can we get that.

First of all, install an app called Web editor: inspect websites in your android phone. Download links are below:

Google Play (3.7 MB): play.google.com/store/ap...

APK Pure (4.5 MB): apkpure.com/web-editor-ins...

 


After Installing, open that app and go to facebook.com.



Now login to your facebook account. Make sure that the account must be unlocked and should be public. Follow step 1 for more details.


After logging in, if the website ask for Login with one tap, you must click on OK. Otherwise the cookie will not work properly.


Now click on the 3 dot icon and Click on Edit cookies.


Now you will see a promt containing some text. You have to copy full text. That is the cookie of your facebook account.


Now get back to TZ Liker settings and paste the cookie/text in the big textarea. And click on Set Cookie.


You are done! You have success fully connected your facebook account with TZ Liker.

Follow next step to see how to get likes.

Step 4: Get Likes on your Post

To get likes on your facebook post-

First of all, open that post and click on 3 dot icon of that post. Then click on Edit Privacy.

Then click on Public. And copy the post link


Now go to Dashboard of TZ Liker, click on Auto Like. Then paste that copied post link in the form and Click on Next.

Then Choose Reactions (eg. Wow, Haha, Love etc) and Reaction Amount. Then click on submit. You will receive likes in a few moment.
